The Indian Space Research Organisation[a] (ISRO; /ˈɪsroʊ/) is the national space agency of India.

The Indian space programme started with INCOSPAR which was established in February 1962.

It was later superseded by ISRO on 15th August 1969, to institutionalize space research in India.
